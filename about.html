About me

Am Mahendra Singh Dhoni (born 7 July 1981), commonly known as MS Dhoni and Mahi, I'm a cricket player and was the former captain of the Indian cricket team. My home town is Ranchi,Jharkhand. I got married on 4th July 2010.I'm a right-handed batsman as well as wicket keeper.I has my own famous cricket shot called the "Helicopter".

Am used to captain the Indian national team in limited-overs formats from 2007 to 2016 and in Test cricket from 2008 to 2014.I has led India to 2007 ICC World Twenty20 victory, the 2010 and 2016 Asia Cup victories, the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup victory and the 2013 ICC Champions Trophy victory. Am also the present captain of Chennai Super Kings in the Indian Premier League.

I am a wicket-keeper batsman and bats in the middle order.I'm one of the few cricketers with more than 10,000 runs in ODIs. Experts deem myself to be one of the best "finishers" in limited-overs formats in World Cricket. Many also regarded me as one of the best wicket-keepers in modern limited-overs international cricket.
